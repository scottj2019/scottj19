Our third rotation was themed around some creative machines in the WIC like the T-Shirt printer and the UV printer.

For the first assignment we had to desgin and print our own t-shirt. The design had to be based on WRA, Hudson, or Ohio. My plan was to create a design of the outline of Ohio with a lot of lines traced from the border of Ohio to the border of Hudson.

The second assignment was to sublimate a design that we made onto some item like a mug or a pencil case. I thought it'd be a cool idea to put some pacman pictures on ceramic coasters.

The third, and final assignment, of the rotation was to print something on the UV printer. One of the options was a puzzle, and my mom loves to do puzzles so I chose to try and print a picture of her dog onto a puzzle.
